Website for the paper EMOTIONET

Repository for the paper EMOTIONET: A Multi-Convolutional Neural Network Hierarchal Approach to Facial and Emotional Classification using TPUs accepted by ICE/IEEE ITMC Conference 2020


EMOTIONET is a CNN hierarchal approach to try to impove results for facial and emotional classification. This project stemmed out from my master's thesis work for my degree at $University. I further expanded the research through tensorflow's research cloud with the help of TPUs.

When the paper is posted online, I will link to it here.

BIG thank you to tensorflow research cloud for making this happen. I have included many links in the nav bar, including my own code for TPU Pods and a custom tensorflow build, and a raspberry pi application of the paper NN I have done!
